# What it is: 
    The neural nexus is a media-processing pipeline and RAG application implementing few-shot prompts with fine-tuned LoRA adapters on a LLM. 

# Why it exists: 
 - be remembered, 
 - know your heritage, 
 - connect with lost loved ones, 
 - communicate with fictional characters, 
 - communicate with the likeness of any public figure (as they are signed up to the neural nexus and choose to share their avatar) (twitter is a one-to-manny conversation; neural nexus is a many-to-many conversation; continue any conversation across any medium at anytime with anyone, even if unavailable)
  
# What it does or will do:
 -  have a personal secretary of yourself to triage and respond to social mediums on your behalf. 
 -  Have a queryable centralized location for the state of your health and well-being through the integration of personal data. 
 -  Connect your social media accounts for passive-uploads of your own personal presence. 

# Future features: 
## Neural Data:
- Thought-to-text neural data and text-to-thought communication; 
- generative images driven by the skeleton of your image; learns your movements and mannerisms (create interactive portraits of yourself and family that will see and interact with you in real-time); 
- connects to neural data from motor cortex via consumer Neuralink in the future when available. 
  
## Augmented reality presence 
- (upload full-body images; create a physical avatar that looks and moves as you in the real-world)

    - another application where people go to places that are marked for avatars; you attend a physical location and that location upon visiting with create a notification that there is an avatar to communicate with)
